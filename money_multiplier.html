Working Capital Cycle (WCC)
WCC Formula: WCC = Debtor Days + Inventory Days − Creditor Days
Debtor Days = (Trade Receivables ÷ Annual Revenue) × 365
Inventory Days = (Inventory ÷ COGS) × 365  |  Creditor Days = (Trade Payables ÷ COGS) × 365
Threshold: 15–60 days. Below 15 = client doesn't need product. Above 60 = cycle too long.

Limit Sizing Ratio (LSR)
LSR Formula: LSR = Requested Credit Limit ÷ Average Monthly Revenue
Threshold: 0.25–0.75. Above 0.75 = oversized (reduce limit). Below 0.25 = undersized.

Repayment Comfort Score (RCS)
RCS Formula: RCS = Average Monthly Net Cash Inflow ÷ Maximum Monthly Repayment Obligation
Max Repayment = Credit Limit + (Rate × Limit in Lakhs × Days)
Threshold: ≥ 1.5 = Pass · 1.0–1.5 = Caution (reduce limit) · < 1.0 = Fail.
